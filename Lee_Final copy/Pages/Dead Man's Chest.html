Jack On The Flying Dutchman

Jack sneaks his way aboard the Dutchman in search of the key to unlock the Dead Man's Chest which contains the beating heart of Davy Jones.

Isla Cruces

Jack figures out how to get to the island using his lucky compass to guide him. He finds the Dead Man's Chest buried in the sand and digs to retrieve. Jack inserts the key into the chest to reveals the beating heart of Davy Jones.

Character Interaction: Calypso

Jack and his crew travel to Calypso's spell shack because she is the one who knows where the key to the Dead Man's Chest is. She gives them a map drawing of the key so they know exactly how it looks when they go to find it on the Flying Dutchman. She has spell items that resemble the features of Davy Jones and she rolls them on to the table to sense where the Dutchman is at sea.
