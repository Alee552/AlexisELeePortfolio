Jack On The Black Pearl

Jack held prisoner by Barbossa in the brig as the crew on deck are preparing to battle the HMS Inceptor ship. Jack helps save Elizabeth Swann from being captive by Barbossa on the Pearl. The moon rises and the crew, as well as, Jack and Barbossa's crew turn into living skeletons due to the Aztec Curse. In the end, Jack then becomes captain of the Black Pearl after Hector Barbossa who becomes his first mate but they are always at each other's throats.

Isla de la Muerta

A chest made from stone originally contained 882 Aztec gold pieces, but stolen by Barbossa in a cave. Eventually, Barbossa is able to collect the gold to replenish the chest, but is missing a piece of gold. Jack finds his way to the island on the Pearl and comes across Barbossa who is about to sacrifice Elizabeth Swann as she has the medallion (missing gold piece) and persuades him that she is of the Turner family bloodline and he believes her lie . Jack intervenes and the moon rises again as Hector, his crew, turn into a living skeletons. Jack steals the medallion and throws it to Will Turner to draw his blood on it to lift the curse.

Character Interaction: Hector Barbossa

Jack and Hector battle each other for the last piece of gold to be added to the stone chest. Eventually, Jack shoots Hector and as the curse is lifted he feels death starting to sink in.
