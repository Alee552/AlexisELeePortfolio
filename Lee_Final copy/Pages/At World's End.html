Jack on The Empress

Jack makes a few brief appearances on the Empress. He is tossed about the ship as the crew battles the British HMS Endeavor. Jack survives the battle on the ship and lives to see another day on the high seas.

Davy Jones' Locker

Jack became trapped in Davy Jones' Locker for refusing to become a member of Davy Jones crew. Jack and the Pearl were placed in a dried up sea in the locker. He starts hallucinating and seeing duplicates of himself with different personalities. All of this eventually makes him feel like he is losing his mind.

Character Interaction: Davy Jones

Jack gets into a sword fight with Davy Jones as he is trying to steal the Dead Man's Chest again. The difference this time is that he stabs Davy Jones' beating heart. Davy Jones' collapses and dies.
